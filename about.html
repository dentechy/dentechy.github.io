Dennis Feliciano is very passionate about nutrition, fitness and technology. Throughout the years, he became very knowledgeable in all three. In college he studied biochemistry and became an ACSM certified personal trainer. In grad school, he further enriched his knowledge on the biochemical aspects of nutrition.

The journey with technology though, began several years ago when he first installed Linux on an old Sony Vaio laptop. Over the years, he would experiment with the command line and eventually enroll in an online course tailored towards Linux system administration. That course provided valuable information on managing his own Linux systems, but doing everything strictly through the command line, even editing files through vim. His current Linux systems include a raspberry pi configured as an offsite backup in CT and another that is used as a local file server.

The progression to web development was only natural from there. He has since completed a full stack web development program, starting with the basics in HTML, CSS and JavaScript and moving to more advanced concepts such as Node.js, Express, mongodb and React.
